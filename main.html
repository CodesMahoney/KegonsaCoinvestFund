Welcome to Kegonsa Coinvest Fund

The Fund is managed by Kegonsa Capital Partners, LLC, a Wisconsin-based venture capital management firm with an early-stage investment focus. The primary Fund objective is investor return. The Fund coinvests with larger scale investment firms, primarily in rounds two, three and four with $15-25M of total paid in capital for portfolio companies and expected exits of $50M-150M.
