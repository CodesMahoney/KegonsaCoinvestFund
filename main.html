Welcome to Kegonsa Coinvest Fund

The Fund is managed by Kegonsa Capital Partners, LLC, a Wisconsin-based venture capital management firm. The primary Fund objective is investor return. The Fund coinvests in rounds led by larger regional and national venture capital firms. The Fund invests primarily in rounds two, three and four with $5-20MM of investment in each round and expected portfolio company exits of $50M-150M.

Disclosure: The Kegonsa Coinvest Fund is not seeking investors in the Fund nor in any portfolio companies. The Kegonsa Coinvest Fund is no longer making investments in new companies
